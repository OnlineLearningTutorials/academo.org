Useful notes

Notice how Croatia looks like a letter C: C for Croatia.

If you struggle to remember the order of the Baltic States (Estonia, Latvia and Lithuania), it can be helpful to remember that they are in alphabetical order moving from North to South.

The most northerly of the Nordic countries is Norway - you can think "Nor(th)way".
